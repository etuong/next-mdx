### These are some of my favorite quotes for a little inspiration

#### I collected these quotes from online resources many moons ago. I apologize I can't provide credits but please note that these are not my own words.

1. To the world you may be just one person, but to one person you may be the world. - Dr. Seuss

1. If you live to be 100, I hope I live to be 100 minus 1 day, so I never have to live without you.

1. Friends are like bras: close to your heart and there for support.

1. A journey of a thousand miles begin with the first step - Laozi

1. Experience is not what happens to a man, it's what he does with what happens to him - Aldous Huxley

1. I am the master of my fate, the captain of my soul. - William Ernest Henley

1. The two most important days of your life are the first day you were born, and the day you find out why - Mark Twain

1. Live as if you were to die tomorrow. Learn as if you were to live forever - Ghandi

1. It's better to look back on life and say, “I can't believe I did that,” than to look back and say, “I wish I did that.” - Marc Chernoff

1. A real friend is one who walks in when the rest of the world walks out.

1. You can lose a lot of money chasing women, but you will never lose women by chasing money

1. Not everything that counts can be counted, and not everything that's counted truly counts. - Albert Einstein

1. We're so anxious about what will happen in the future that we don't enjoy the present. The result being, we don't live in the present or the future. We end up living feeling like we're never going to die, and then die never having truly lived.
   The most powerful prison is one when you don't even know you're locked up. - Jay Shetty

1. Engineering is a great profession. There is the fascination of watching a figment of the imagination emerge through the aid of science to a plan on paper. Then it moves to realization in stone or metal or energy. Then it brings homes to men and women. Then it elevates the standard of living and adds to the comforts of life. This is the engineer's high privilege. — Herbert Clark Hoover, engineer & U.S. president from 1929-1932

1. Returning violence for violence only multiplies violence, adding deeper to a night already devoid of stars. Darkness cannot drive out darkness; only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate; only love can do that. - Doctor Martin Luther King

1. Practice positive thinking; don't be half-empty, be half-full.

1. Don't forget who you are, you're a nobody if you don't know where you come from. - Ethan Uong

1. There comes a time in your life, when you walk away from all the drama and people who create it. You surround yourself with people who make you laugh. Forget the bad, and focus on the good. Love the people who treat you right, pray for the ones who don't. Life is too short to be anything but happy. Falling down is a part of life, getting back up is living.
